Privacy Policy — Coherence

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Please read alongside our Terms of Service. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights. Coherence is a wellness product, not a medical service.

Who we are

Coherence ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Spectiv FZ L.L.C, Dubai, UAE. For any privacy question or request, contact us at info@spectivmedia.co.

The short version

  • We collect the minimum we need to run the app: your account details, your readings (heart-rate-variability data derived from your phone camera), your practice activity, and anything you choose to post in the community.
  • Your health-related data (your readings and derived scores) is used to give you your results and trends. It is first-party — we do not sell it, and we do not share it with advertisers.
  • You can delete your account and data at any time from within the app.

What we collect

Account information. When you sign in (e.g. with Apple), we receive an identifier and, if you provide it, your email. We store a display name if you set one.

Reading data (health-related). When you take a reading, the app measures your pulse from your fingertip on the camera and derives heart-rate-variability metrics and the scores/states shown to you. We store your readings and derived values so you can see your history and trends. We do not store video from your camera; the camera signal is processed into pulse metrics.

Practice & meditation activity. Which guided practices you start and complete, and for how long, so we can show you your activity and improve the practices.

Community content. If you use community features, we store what you post, your friend connections, and reports you make or receive.

Support interactions. If you contact support or use in-app support chat, we process your messages to help you. Support-chat messages may be processed by a third-party AI provider (see "Third parties") to generate a response.

Technical/basic usage data. Basic information needed to operate and secure the app.

We do not collect precise location, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Why we use it (and our legal bases, where applicable)

  • To provide the core service — your readings, scores, trends, and practices (performance of our contract with you).
  • To operate community and support features (our legitimate interests and/or your consent).
  • To keep the service safe and prevent abuse (legitimate interests / legal obligations).
  • To improve the product in aggregate (legitimate interests).

Sensitive/health-related data & your consent

Your readings and derived scores relate to your body and wellbeing and may be considered health-related data under some laws. By using the reading features you consent to us processing this data to provide your results. This data is used to serve you your results and, in aggregate and de-identified where possible, to improve the product. It is never sold and never shared with advertisers.

Third parties we use

We share data only with service providers who help us run the app, under appropriate safeguards:

  • InstantDB — our database/backend, which stores your account and app data.
  • Apple — sign-in and (for subscriptions) payment processing. We do not receive your full payment details.
  • Anthropic — processes support-chat messages you send, solely to generate support responses.
  • Our hosting provider(s) — to run our servers.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers.

International transfers

We operate across multiple regions and use service providers that may process data in countries other than yours. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

How long we keep it

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your account and associated data as described below.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these, use the in-app controls or contact info@spectivmedia.co.

Deleting your account. You can delete your account and its data at any time from within the app (Profile → Delete account). This removes your account and associated records.

Children

Coherence is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children under that age.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the "last updated" date and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.
