Coherence is a wellness app that reads your heart rhythm through your phone's camera to reflect your nervous-system state. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep data collection lean and first-party — we do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Who we are

Coherence ("we", "us") is provided by [Your Company / Legal Entity]. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at [SUPPORT_EMAIL].

Information we collect

  • Account & profile. A name, age, and biological sex you enter at onboarding (age and sex are used only to choose the right heart-rate-variability reference range). If you create an account, your email address and sign-in provider (Apple or email).
  • Your readings. The results of each reading — heart-rate-variability metrics, a coherence score, a nervous-system state, and the reflective "energy center" values — plus the time of the reading. These are wellness reflections, not medical measurements.
  • Practice & app usage. Which guided practices and meditations you start and complete and for how long, and basic in-app feedback (a rating and optional note) you choose to leave.
  • Community content. If you post to the community feed, the content you share (a reading snapshot or message), comments, reactions, and any reports you make.
  • Support messages. If you use in-app support chat, the messages you send.
  • Technical basics. Information needed to run the app reliably, such as an anonymous device identifier for your account session.

Camera and motion sensors

To take a reading, Coherence uses your camera to detect the subtle colour changes at your fingertip as your pulse passes through it, and your device's motion sensor to sense stillness during a reading. The camera frames are processed on your device to compute a pulse signal; we do not store or transmit raw camera video or photographs. The camera and motion sensors are used only during a reading.

How we use your information

  • To calculate and show your readings, scores, trends, and reflective insights.
  • To learn your personal baseline over time so your scores are fair to you.
  • To power features you choose to use — practices, the community feed, and support chat.
  • To understand, in aggregate, which practices are associated with better readings, so we can improve the app.
  • To keep the service secure and working correctly.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

How your data is stored and shared

Your data is stored in your private account on our backend database provider, InstantDB, which processes and hosts data on our behalf. We share information only with the service providers needed to run the app:

  • InstantDB — database and account hosting.
  • Anthropic — if you use in-app support chat, your message is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a reply. Don't include sensitive personal details in support chat.
  • Apple — for Sign in with Apple and, if you subscribe, for processing payments through the App Store. Payments are handled by Apple; we do not receive your card details.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete account; this permanently removes your profile, readings, practice history, community content, and other data owned by your account. Some records may persist briefly in backups before being overwritten.

Your choices and rights

  • Access and update your profile details in the app.
  • Delete your account and associated data in the app.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, or a copy of your data). Contact us at [SUPPORT_EMAIL] to make a request.

Children

Coherence is not intended for anyone under 13, and you must be at least 13 to create an account. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Not a medical service

Coherence is a wellness tool for reflection. It does not diagnose, treat, or monitor any medical condition, and it is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll change the "last updated" date above, and for material changes we'll provide notice in the app.
